Join us for New Year's Eve!

Swing in the new roaring '20s with us at the 10th Street Station in Ford City, PA. Doors open at 8:00 pm with the amazing Dr. Zoot playing until 12:30 am.

"What can I expect at the party?" Complimentary beer, wine, and a midnight champagne toast along with traditional New Year's Eve appetizers! All other alcohol is BYOB. We will also have a balloon drop, wine pull, silent auction, and more! Check the bottom of this page for a more detailed schedule of events.

"Where can I get tickets?" Tickets are available for purchase at Klingensmith's Drug Stores in Ford City, West Kittanning, and Leechburg. They can also be purchased on our Facebook page (at this link) or at Eventbrite (at this link) ($50 + online fees for single tickets).

We are also offering the chance for businesses and individuals to sponsor tables at the event! Each sponsored table includes 8 individual tickets, a reserved table, and prominent thanks as a sponsor of the event!

"Who is throwing this New Year's Eve party?" All proceeds will benefit revitalization efforts of the building at the corner of Fifth Avenue and Ford Street in Ford City. We are calling the development "Fifth and Ford", and we are incorporated with Pennsylvania as "Constellations LLC."

See you there!

Who is Dr. Zoot?

Dr. Zoot is a full band, covering a hundred years of dance music -- from swing to polka to new favorites and everything in between. Learn more at their website (here), watch a sample of their music on YouTube (here), or add them to your Spotify favorites (here).
