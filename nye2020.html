Join us for New Years Eve!

Swing in the new roaring '20s with us at the 10th Street Station in Ford City, PA. Doors open at 8:00 pm with the amazing Dr. Zoot playing until 12:30 am. Ticket price ($50 each) includes - unlimited beer, wine, traditional New Years appetizers, and a midnight champagne toast! All other alcohol is BYOB. Tickets are available for purchase at Klingensmith's Drug Stores in Ford City, West Kittanning, and Leechburg. They can also be purchased online at Eventbrite (at this link) or on our Facebook page (at this link) ($50 + online fees for single tickets).

We are also offering the chance for businesses and individuals to sponsor tables at the event! Each sponsored table includes 8 individual tickets, a reserved table, and prominent thanks as a sponsor of the event!

All proceeds will benefit revitalization efforts of the building at the corner of Fifth Avenue and Ford Street in Ford City. See you there!

As we add more details about the New Years Eve party, we will post them here and to the event on Facebook!
